The Memory of My Cat, Fatty Mc Fat Fat

I want to dedicate the memory of my cat Fatty Mc Fat Fat. However, this site will be for other folks who had similar big fat orange cats and want to share their stories of their cats with other people. I want this site to be comforting and peaceful. An enjoyable experience for those folks who are heartbroken about losing their fat orange cats to old age
